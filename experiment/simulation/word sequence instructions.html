Instructions for Word Sequence Experiment

1. On the next screen,you will be shown a list of words.

2. Try to remember these words visually and mentally.

3. You will have only 8 seconds to look at the word list and and remember them,after which the screen will automatically move to the next task.

4. A timer as shown on the right will indicate the time elapsed.

5. When you are ready, click on the START button.

6. Once clicked,you will not be able to return to this page till the experiment is over.
